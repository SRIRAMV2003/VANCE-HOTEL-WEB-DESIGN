Located in Gowanus, Brooklyn, Vance serves Authentic tapas from Catalonia in a modern industrial space.
We also serve beer, wine and spirits.


Tapas originated in the Catalonian region of Spain and are small plates typically
consumed with wine or vermouth.

We combine traditional preparations with local ingredients for dishes that are authentic to both
their Spanish origins and New York setting.
